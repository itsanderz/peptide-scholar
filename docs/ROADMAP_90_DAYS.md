# 90-Day Execution Roadmap

Date: April 11, 2026
Purpose: Convert the strategy and blueprint into a tightly sequenced, parallel 90-day build plan.

## 1. Goals For The First 90 Days

- establish market-aware architecture
- improve conversion on existing high-intent pages
- launch two new core tools
- ship provider matcher foundations
- create the schema and pSEO system for controlled scale
- prepare the app/PWA funnel without overbuilding it

## 2. Workstreams

### A. Platform
- market model
- review states
- source registry
- content governance

### B. CRO and UX
- homepage
- nav
- CTA framework
- treatment/legal/comparison templates

### C. pSEO Engine
- schemas
- generation contract
- validation
- index rules

### D. Revenue Ops
- provider matcher
- CRM mapping
- partner attribution

### E. Tools and App
- half-life visualizer
- vial depletion calculator
- app landing pages
- PWA shell decisions

## 4. Owners

- Platform lead: market model, route logic, governance
- Growth PM: roadmap, funnel, KPI review
- Design lead: homepage, nav, CTA system, templates
- SEO lead: schemas, batch launches, indexing policy
- Partnerships lead: provider matcher logic, partner QA, lead review
- App/tools lead: new tools and PWA prep

## 5. KPI Targets

90-day directional targets:
- provider CTA CTR up materially on top entry pages
- tool completion > 35%
- email capture rate up on money pages
- quick-back and dead-click reduction on homepage/legal pages
- first pSEO batch indexed at acceptable rate

## 6. Hard Rules

- do not expand page count before first batches show quality and conversion
- do not ship market pages without market-aware CTA logic
- do not ship provider pages without lead handling and attribution
- do not start native app work in this 90-day window
